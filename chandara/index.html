Who am I

Hello everyone, my name is Chandara Seth (in Khmer: Seth Chandara). I am 16 years old. I was born in a province in Cambodia called Kampong Chhnang.     Quality of Education

    Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports, Cambodia has rated American Intercon Schools System (AIS) the best in its nationwide school inspection. Also, for two years in a row, AIS has topped the ranking of schools in Cambodia in the National High School Exit Exams, also with highest passing rate; as declared by Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports, Cambodia. Because of such accomplishments, AIS now boasts to have the most number of students studying overseas after graduating from AIS. AIS students have also been highlighted in numerous national and regional platforms showcasing the realization of its vision, “Every student at AIS graduates with the best quality education possible in the country.”
